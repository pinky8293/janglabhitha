Our Curriculum

At Jangla Bhitha School, we believe in providing a holistic education that empowers students with both knowledge and essential life skills. Our curriculum is designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and a love for learning.

Nursery to Class 5

Our foundational curriculum is designed to provide a strong base for early education, focusing on play-based learning and creativity. Subjects include Language (Hindi, English), Mathematics, and General Knowledge.

Class 6 to Class 8

At this level, we prepare students for secondary education. The curriculum includes subjects like Mathematics, Science, Social Science, English, Hindi, and Computer Science. We focus on practical education and project-based learning to apply concepts.

Class 8 to Class 10

Students strengthen their foundation in Mathematics, Science, Social Science, and Languages. They also develop problem-solving, analytical, and critical thinking skills for board exams.

Class 10 to Class 12

Students choose specialized streams like Science, Commerce, or Arts for higher studies. They gain in-depth subject knowledge and prepare for college, careers, and competitive exams.

Extra-Curricular Activities

We encourage a wide range of activities beyond academics to ensure the all-round development of our students. This includes sports, music, art, and debate clubs, helping them explore their talents and interests.
