About Us

Principal's Message

Dear Parents, Students, and Teachers, welcome to Jangla Bhitha School. Our mission is not only to strengthen students academically but also to equip them with moral values and life skills. We believe in creating an environment where every student can reach their full potential. We welcome your active participation in shaping the future of your children.

School Rules & Regulations

At our school, discipline and respect are of utmost importance. All students must arrive on time, wear neat uniforms, and show respect to teachers. The use of mobile phones in classrooms is strictly prohibited.
